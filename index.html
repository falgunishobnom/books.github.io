Arthur Conan Doyle

Arthur Conan Doyle is a world renown crime novels author, with over 130 years of success under his belt!

Arthur Conan Doyle was born in 1859 in Edinburgh, among Scottish mountainscapes... Ironically, this gifted penman pursued a well-established career of a doctor. But exactly during his college years, he began to do his first writings. It all began when he released his first short stories. Such as "The Mystery of Sasassa Valley" and "The Haunted Grange of Goresthorpe". That introduced him to notable publishers right at the beginning of his career. Eventually, that helped him to see a way to get his breakthrough piece ("Adventures of Sherlock Holmes") published! He inked that book while waiting for customers in his medical physician's office. But it was not until 1891 when his first book "Adventures of Sherlock Holmes" has been published. Since then, enjoying a career of a famous writer since, he penned almost 20 more full scaled novel books under his name!
